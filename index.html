About Your Coach

Built by the game.
Proven at every level.

Caleb George is a former collegiate and semi-professional soccer player raised through Tampa Bay United's academy before earning a full scholarship to FGCU, where he won back-to-back Atlantic Sun Championships and made three NCAA Tournament appearances.

After college, Caleb competed at the semi-professional level, suiting up for Tampa Bay Rowdies U-23 (PDL) against MLS opponents Chicago Fire and NYCFC, then Kaw Valley FC (Sporting KC affiliate), Lakeland Tropics (USL League Two), and more. He brings that experience directly to every session.
